How to Play:
Setup:
• Buy and place pieces from your inventory
• White pawns must be placed on bottom 3 rows
• Cannot place pieces that check either king
• Once placed, pieces are locked (use Reset to clear all)
• Must place your King before starting!
Battle:
• Click your pieces to move them on your turn
• Capture black pieces to earn points (½ piece value)
• Win: +3 points, Draw: +1 point
Progression:
• Surviving pieces return to original positions
• Black gets stronger each round (1.5x after wins, 1x after draws)
• Black pieces spawn anywhere (pawns: top 3 rows)
• Win 10 games to achieve victory!
